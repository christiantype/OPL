Faculty

Faculty 2 was the sophomore issue of the Chicago Graphic Design Club's publication — a beacon of creative voices spanning across disciplines and perspectives.

The publication represents a collaborative effort developed alongside Rick Valicenti. The partnership emphasizes shared values in authorship, experimentation, and cultural responsibility, producing work reflecting both content rigor and design's critical potential.
