Apply to Reside

A residency at OPL is not a job. It is an invitation into a shared space of attention — a place where designers, thinkers, and makers come to do work that refuses to arrive quietly.

Residents are named, present, and accountable to the practice. They bring their own questions and leave with work that belongs to something larger than themselves. OPL is a collective with a rotating cast, held together not by a single personality but by a shared ethos.

If you are drawn to design as inquiry — as a form of consciousness, not just production — we want to hear from you.
